    About Us

    We are a dedicated team of G12 students from STI College Marikina, driven by a passion for technology and a shared vision to empower culinary businesses. With our combined skills in programming, data management, and creative problem-solving, we have embarked on a journey to create a Sales and Inventory Management System that will make a significant impact in the culinary industry. Our mission is to provide culinary establishments with a user-friendly and efficient tool that not only simplifies their daily operations but also helps them make informed decisions based on real-time sales and inventory data. We are committed to delivering a system that can optimize processes, reduce waste, and ultimately contribute to the success of our clients in the culinary world. As we work on this project for our expo program, we are excited about the opportunity to apply our knowledge, gain real-world experience, and make a positive difference in the culinary sector. We look forward to sharing our progress and the final product with the community and industry partners. Thank you for your support and interest in our endeavor."
